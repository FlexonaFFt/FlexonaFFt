I wanted to understand it deeply, not just skim the surface. So I learn by doing: I compete in hackathons, place on leaderboards, break things, rebuild them, and ship working solutions. Two first-place finishes at the Southern IT Forum, top results at Yandex ML contests and Archipelag — each one taught me more than any course could. Right now I'm building an adaptive learning platform powered by IRT-based ML models (went through an accelerator, deploying at a university) and an intelligent support bot with RAG. I surround myself with people who push me forward, and I keep moving.
